what are the benefits?

Lowers stress and symtoms

Stress is associated with the release of many inflammatory chemicals called cytokines. There has been many research that shows meditation relieves the body of stress and related symtoms.

Enhances mental health

Regular meditation can lead to higher self-confidence and a positive outlook on life, which in turn reduces the negative thoughts that can occur during daily life.

Lower blood pressure

By easing stress and the strain on your heart, meditation is known for lowering blood pressure which can also reduce the risk of a heart attack and stroke.

Increases concentration

Meditation can increase focus and the duration of the attention span, this will aid many areas of life such as work or school.

Reduce memory loss

Studies show meditation can prevent age-related memory loss, when tested those who meditate regulary have excellent mental clarity conpared to others in their age bracket.

Improves sleep hygiene

If you struggle with sleep, then meditation can help you achieve deep sleep faster as it is known for easing the body and achieving a peaceful state.
